The Beginning Of Our Story

Hello everyone. here we will tell you about our love story. the story is started when we were in an orientation event on campus. we met each other because we are in the same group.

Andrian's POV

The first time I saw her is when we are in high school, it may be every morning I saw her but she was with her Boyfriend. At the time I saw her, I thought "she was beautiful". When I started my studies on campus at orientation time, I see her again and the delightful thing is we are in the same group.

Compliment

In college, we rarely see each other, but in the last semester, a started to send her a message cause she changed her profile picture in BBM, to give a compliment about her hair.

First Date

After I gave a compliment to her, I always keep in touch with her. the momentum is perfect with the launching of the insidious film at the cinema, so I invited her to go to the cinema. After we watched the film, our relationship were started.

Kanti's POV

the first time I saw him was when we were in the orientation time at campus, we both had been given a punishment by the committee due to less use of attributes. when were being punished I realize that we are from the same high school.

First Impression

After orientation, our communication has stopped. At the moment I must build good relations with a lot of people including him even though he is at different faculty. But after a couple of times, we met at the campus, I thought he's an icy person.

The Beginning

I forgot how it start, but at the moment our communication is intense. but at the time we still had a relationship, I had a boyfriend, he has a girlfriend.

The Started

2016 when i changed my profil picture on my BBM Account, he started chatting with me, after a lot of communication , he invited me to watch a film at the cinema, and it was our first date.

The Surprising Thing

we make a relationship, our new story started from this point. after a long time we were at the same ways, the funny thing's come we were at the same schools from Junior High School, Senior High School, and also at the same campus. and now we were work at the same Company

2019

Around 3 years we are in this relationship, we decided to get engagement, is it a massive decision that we haven't made before, we never imagine that things will come to us, sometimes there are many questions in our head that we have made a good decision or not. But, we don't know how to tell you what we felt. The point is we felt happy.

The Journey

Our relations are not always in the right ways, there are many mistakes, different perceptions, and many more, but we always find ways to solve the problems, I feel so glad to make a relationship with her, the beautiful, girl who can make me better and better, the girl who can be patient facing a childish man. After many problems, we faced together and many think passing by, the moments come, I decided to marry her.

The End and The New Start

After we get engagment for 2 years, finally we decided to get marry. We hope our decision is right. We hope after we marry, we can be better and better again. We hope our decision is right. We hope after we marry, we can be better and better again.
